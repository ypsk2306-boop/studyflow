StudyFlow Quick Documentation

Learn how to utilize StudyFlow's premium features to maximize your academic output.

How do I bind study sessions to specific tasks?
Head to the **Focus Room** tab. In the right panel, look at the **Bind to Active Task** selector. Choose an active task from the list, and when your Pomodoro countdown finishes, the statistics (like completed Pomodoros tally) will be updated directly to that task on the **Study Planner** board!
What are the commands available in the Notes editor?
Type / on a new block line to reveal the slash command menu. You can insert headings (H1, H2, H3), lists (Bulleted, Checklists), callouts (for highlights), pre-formatted code syntax blocks, and structural dividers. Pressing Enter inserts a new text block, and Backspace on a blank line deletes it.
How are study streaks calculated?
Streaks are calculated based on your daily Pomodoro focus sessions. If you complete at least one focus session today or yesterday, your streak will keep burning! If you go a full day without studying, the streak count resets to 0.
Is my data stored securely online?
Currently, StudyFlow is fully client-side. Your accounts, tasks, sessions, and private notes are stored locally in your browser's private `localStorage` database, meaning it is not synced to third-party servers. Passwords are cryptographically hashed using **SHA-256** to prevent plaintext leaks.

About StudyFlow Workspace

Learn more about StudyFlow's key features, design, and architecture.

StudyFlow Workspace

StudyFlow is a premium study companion designed to empower students to build disciplined habits, organize coursework, and maximize focus. Combining task planning, deep work timers, ambient soundtracks, and beautiful analytics, StudyFlow provides a cohesive local workstation for your academic success.

Core Workspace Features

Study Planner & Kanban

Organize your assignments and study topics using a flexible board. Assign priority tags, estimated Pomodoros, and drag-and-drop tasks across workflow columns.

Focus Room & Ambient Mixer

Dive into deep work with a Pomodoro timer backed by breathing animations. Customize your focus experience using a custom ambient mixer featuring Rain, Parisian Cafe, Binaural Waves, and Forest soundtracks.

Block Notes Workspace

Compose your study notes with a rich text editor supporting block commands. Type / to insert headings, lists, checkbox tasks, info callouts, code syntax boxes, and dividers.

Productivity Analytics

Visualize your focus progress. StudyFlow tracks your focus streaks, logs your daily study achievements, and plots subject breakdowns and weekly distributions using native SVG charts.

Technology Stack & Architecture

StudyFlow is engineered using fully client-side technologies to guarantee offline performance, data security, and instant response times:

  • Structure & Style: Semantic HTML5 and CSS variables with full themes support (Dark, Light, Cozy Sepia).
  • Application Logic: Vanilla ESM Javascript modules for component state management, router, and UI synchronization.
  • Local Database: Multi-profile browser local storage integration with cryptographically hashed passwords (SHA-256).
